PixMMVP and PixCV-Bench Paired Evaluation Benchmark for Visual Grounding and Visual Question Answering

Abstract

This work is part of a series that tries to push pixel-level visual grounding in images and videos to improve without degrading their fundamental chat capabilities.

The series is focused on paired evaluation where a visual grounding task is paired with a respective task that helps us understand the failures of MLLMs directed towards pixel-level understanding.
